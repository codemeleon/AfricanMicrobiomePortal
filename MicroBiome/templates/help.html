Search

This is the home page. It is divided in to two pannels: Pannel A is Search forms and pannel B is the graphical overview of the data in the database.

Search form accepts multiple keywords, seprated with tab key. Empty search form for all the results.

Overview pannel is divided into 5 sub-pannels providing graphical overview of sample collection geographical location, heath contions of individuals, sequencing platform, sample collection body site and assay used in the study.

Query results are provided as table. However, due to large number of columns, most of the information are hidden and cab accessed by clicking "+" on the left of each row. Result can also be acquired using imbedded links in the summary pannel. Result can be further filtered using the form on the right top corner of the table.
